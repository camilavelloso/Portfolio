Hi, I'm Camila. I'm a web designer and reporter based in Washington D.C.

Web design & development

I designed and coded two projects for the Howard Center for Investigative Journalism using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, jQuery and JavaScript.

"Code Red" is an investigative project that explores the effects of rising temperatures on the lives and health of Baltimore's urban heat island residents. The project was a collaboration among the Howard Center for Investigative Journalism, Capital News Service and NPR.

"Growing Up Behind Bars"" is an investigative project that examines the state of juvenile justice in the United States, the only country to condemn people to life in prison without parole for crimes committed as minors. The project was a collaboration among the Howard Center for Investigative Journalism, Capital News Service and PBS NewsHour.
